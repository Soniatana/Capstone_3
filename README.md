📊 West Region Sales Analysis Dashboard
Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes sales performance for the West Region of EmporiUm. The report compares store and online sales, tracks monthly trends, identifies top-performing product categories, and highlights top-selling books and authors to support business decision-making.

Objectives
Analyze West Region sales performance.
Compare Store Sales and Online Sales.
Monitor monthly sales trends.
Identify top-selling books and authors.
Explore product category performance using interactive filters.
