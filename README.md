📊 West Region Sales Analysis Dashboard
Overview


This Power BI dashboard analyzes sales performance for the West Region of EmporiUm. It compares store and online sales, tracks monthly trends, identifies top-performing product categories, and highlights top-selling books and authors to support data-driven business decisions.


Objectives
Analyze West Region sales performance.
Compare Store Sales and Online Sales.
Monitor monthly sales trends.
Identify top-selling books and authors.
Evaluate product category performance using interactive filters.
